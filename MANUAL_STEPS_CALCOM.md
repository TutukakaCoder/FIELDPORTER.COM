# Cal.com Manual Setup Steps

## 1. Change Event Duration to 30 Minutes

**Goal:** Ensure bookings are 30 minutes long.

1.  Log in to [Cal.com](https://cal.com/).
2.  Go to **Event Types**.
3.  Click on **Discovery Call**.
4.  Change **Duration** from `15` to **30** minutes.
5.  Click **Save**.

## 2. Enable Microsoft Teams for Dynamic Links

**Goal:** Generate a unique Microsoft Teams link for every meeting automatically.
_(Note: Requires a Microsoft Work or School account)_

1.  **Install the App:**
    - Go to **Apps** (in the sidebar).
    - Search for **Microsoft Teams** (under "Conferencing").
    - Click **Install** / **Add**.
    - Follow the login prompt to connect your Microsoft account.

2.  **Configure the Event:**
    - Go back to **Event Types** > **Discovery Call**.
    - Scroll to the **Location** section.
    - Click the dropdown (currently might say "Cal Video" or "Link meeting").
    - Select **Microsoft Teams**.
    - Click **Save**.

Now, every time someone books, Cal.com will automatically create a unique Teams meeting and send the link in the invite.

## 3. Resolve Spam Policy Warning (If present)

1.  Check your Dashboard notifications.
2.  If you see a Google Spam Policy warning, click **Resolve**.
3.  Follow the prompts to verify your email identity.

---

**Done!** Your booking system is now fully configured.
