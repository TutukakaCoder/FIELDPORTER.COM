# Premium Copywriting Overhaul Report

## Objective

Update website content to sound human, premium, and focused on solving painful business problems ("painkillers") rather than offering generic tech upgrades ("vitamins").

## Changes Implemented

### 1. Hero Section (`components/homepage/hero-section.tsx`)

- **Headline:** Changed from "Build Your Own AI Advantage" to **"Cut Overhead. Scale Faster."**
- **Subhead:** Changed from "Master the tools..." to **"Stop hiring for busywork. We build the AI systems that automate your operations and protect your margins."**
- **Goal:** Immediately address the pain of high overhead and slow scaling.

### 2. Services Section (`components/homepage/services-section.tsx`)

- **Section Header:** Changed to **"Stop Lagging. Start Leading."** with description **"We don't sell 'AI'. We sell outcomes."**
- **Service Rebranding:**
  - **Strategic Research** → **"Stop Guessing."** (Tagline: "Market intelligence that kills risk before you spend.")
  - **Rapid Development** → **"Validate Instantly."** (Tagline: "Turn 'good ideas' into working products. Fast.")
  - **Workflow Automation** → **"Cut the Bloat."** (Tagline: "Replace expensive admin hours with reliable code.")
  - **AI Training** → **"Own the Tech."** (Tagline: "Don't rent your advantage. Build internal capability.")

### 3. Trust Indicators (`components/homepage/trust-indicator-bar.tsx`)

- Updated stats to be more concrete and punchy:
  - "15+ Production Systems"
  - "Days Not Months" (Strategy)
  - "No Vendor Lock-in"
  - "100% Founder Led"

### 4. About Section (`components/about/company-foundation.tsx`)

- **Headline:** **"The Engine Behind The Results"**
- **Content:** Clarified the "We vs I" dynamic.
  - Explicitly stated: "FIELDPORTER is a specialized network led by Freddy Hopkins."
  - Explained the model: "I lead the strategy... network handles the heavy lifting."
  - Benefit: "Move faster than traditional agencies while maintaining the personal attention of a boutique partner."
